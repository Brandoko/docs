## Example Apps

It is useful to look at examples of apps built with electron-react-boilerplate to get an idea of how to scale past the initial foundation of the app. Here are some examples of fairly-sized open source apps built with electron-react-boilerplate

* [Popcorn Time Desktop](https://github.com/amilajack/popcorn-time-desktop)
* [DBGlass](https://github.com/web-pal/DBGlass)
* [Channels](https://github.com/BuckyMaler/channels)
* [ivideo](https://github.com/phobal/ivideo)
* [GDLauncher](https://github.com/gorilla-devs/GDLauncher)
* [Tezori](https://github.com/Cryptonomic/Tezori)
